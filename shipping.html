Returns

30-Day Free Returns

Unworn and unwashed items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. We cover return shipping on all eligible orders. To initiate a return, contact us with your order number.

Exchanges

Size Exchanges

Not the right size? We'll exchange it. See our Size Guide before ordering, and get in touch if you need a different size. Exchange orders are dispatched within 2 business days of receiving the return.

Customs & Duties

International Orders

For international orders, customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's import regulations. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping fees.
