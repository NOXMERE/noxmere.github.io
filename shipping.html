Additional Information

Before You Receive It

I

Preparation

All orders are prepared individually before dispatch. Delivery estimates begin once preparation has been completed.

II

Tracking

A tracking reference is provided at the time of dispatch. Updates are available directly through the carrier.

III

Customs

For international destinations, customs duties and import taxes may apply. These are the responsibility of the recipient.

IV

Demand

During periods of increased demand, delivery times may vary slightly beyond the estimates provided.

Returns

Client Assistance

Unworn and unwashed items may be returned within 14 days of delivery. To initiate a return, contact us with your order reference.

Exchanges

Size Exchanges

We accommodate size exchanges on eligible pieces. Consult our Size Guide before placing your order, and contact us if an exchange is required.

Customs & Duties

International Orders

Customs duties and import taxes may apply to international orders depending on the destination country's regulations. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient.
