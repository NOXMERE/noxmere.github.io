Products & Materials

Structured fabric selected for permanence and form. Weight retained through repeated wear.

A singular tone, retained intentionally. One colour. Considered once, kept permanently.

Reactive dyes set at the fibre level. Cold wash, inside out, no machine heat. Colour holds. Any softening over time is natural — most consider it a mark of use.

Sizing & Fit

The fit is deliberate — neither oversized nor constructed. Those preferring structure size down. Refer to the Size Guide for exact measurements.

Pre-washed during production. Cold wash, air dry. Dimensions are retained.

Orders & Payment

All major cards accepted. PayPal and Apple Pay available. Transactions handled via SSL encryption.

Modifications accepted within two hours of placement. After that, the order moves to fulfilment. Contact privately without delay if changes are required.

All orders arrive in the signature matte box. A personal note may be added at checkout. No additional cost.

Returns & Exchanges

If the garment arrives with a production defect, we will replace it entirely — at no cost to you. Contact us privately with a photograph within 14 days of delivery.

Each piece is made to order. We do not require the garment to be returned. If you are unsatisfied, a partial refund will be issued at our discretion. We recommend consulting the Size Guide carefully before ordering.
