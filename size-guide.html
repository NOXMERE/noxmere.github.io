SIZE GUIDE

Fit & Measurements

Find Your Perfect Fit

Our fit is intentionally considered — not too relaxed, not too fitted. Use this guide to find your size with confidence.

Before You Begin

How to Measure Yourself

For best results, use a soft measuring tape. Measure directly over light clothing or bare skin. Have someone assist you for the most accurate readings.

01

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal and parallel to the floor. Keep two fingers between the tape and your body for comfort.

02

Shoulders

Measure from the edge of one shoulder across the back to the edge of the other. Stand naturally — don't hunch or pull your shoulders back.

03

Sleeve Length

Measure from the top of your shoulder down to your wrist, with your arm slightly bent at the elbow.

Fit Philosophy

"Designed to be worn exactly as intended."

For a True-to-Size Fit

Order your usual size. The garment will sit slightly relaxed across the chest with a hem that falls below the hip — our intended silhouette for this piece.

For a Cleaner Silhouette

Size down by one. The fit becomes more structured across the shoulders, still comfortable but with a sharper, more tailored appearance.

Fabric & Shrinkage

Our 400gsm fleece is pre-washed. Minimal shrinkage occurs with cold-wash care. High-heat drying may reduce length by 1–2 cm — we recommend air-drying.

Between Sizes?

If you're between two sizes, consider how you prefer to wear it. Relaxed drape → go larger. Defined fit → go smaller. Both work beautifully with this cut.
